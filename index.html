Welcome to Royal Dragons

Welcome to Royal Dragons, a friendly and welcoming club where everyone can feel safe, included, and have fun. We are a hangout group first, where people come together to relax, laugh, and enjoy the game - but we also offer many exciting activities!

The club was founded on June 1st, 2020, by a brave leader - Athena Sparrowstar (Liz) , later joined by Phoebe (Pho) Highhunter, Siri (Stjerne) Raincake and Vanessa (Pinky) Gloomberg, in the magical land of Misty Valley, Jorvik. Since then, it has grown into a happy and safe space for all our members.

🐎 Western Drill – ride in patterns as a team
🎠 Dressage – show your horse’s skill and grace
🏇 Racing – speed across the land like the wind
🎭 Roleplay – join us on adventures with our rp stable, Wyrmsgate Stables

We hold official events 4–5 days a week, with a mix of games, training, and fun challenges. Our amazing instructors help players of all levels learn and grow in drill and dressage, host rp lessons through our lesson program, and make room for cosy game- and movie nights.

Even though we focus mostly on having fun and spending time together, we also partake in different competitions. You can choose whether you'd like to show off your elegant dressage skills by competing in a dressage routine, or grab the horn and partake in a drill routine.

No matter what you enjoy most, there’s a place for you here. In RD, you can find your forever home. You'll meet people from all over Norway, and participate in different meetups if you'd like to meet your besties in real life. Whether you're looking for friends, fun, or a little adventure - the gates of Royal Dragons are always open.

Come ride with us, and become part of the legend! 🐉
